Football Tournaments

Sandoz Arts and Sports Club has a rich history of organizing successful football tournaments, having hosted ten successful editions. The most recent tournament took place in 2024, marking a decade of fostering sportsmanship and community spirit through football. Each event is marked by the presence of renowned personalities during the inauguration ceremony, lending prestige and excitement to the occasion. Additionally, our club enjoys strong support from our dedicated viewers, whose enthusiasm contributes to the success and vibrancy of our events.

Carrom Tournament

In 2023, Sandoz Arts and Sports Club organized a carrom tournament that was both exciting and well-received. The event brought together players and enthusiasts for a thrilling competition, highlighting our commitment to fostering camaraderie and sportsmanship within the community
